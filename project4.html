Welcome to the anime called "Naruto"

The main character of Naruto anime is the titular protagonist Uzumaki Naruto,
a young ninja from the village of Konohagakure.
The character Uzumaki Naruto is one of the main reasons for why Naruto anime is so popular,
as fans fell in love with this excellently written and inspirational character

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi is renowned throughout the Naruto world for the use of his Sharingan, which he received from his teammate Obito Uchiha,
and is revered as the ninja who has copied over a thousand jutsus, earning him the moniker "Copy Ninja Kakashi"

Naruto-Sasuke

Naruto:

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto.
It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage,
the leader of his village.

Sasuke:

Sasuke with a role as important as the protagonist and a remarkably crafted personality,
the Naruto anime would've never been the same without Sasuke.
Since Naruto's debut, the titular character has always remained at the top of popularity charts
and is highly regarded as one of the most lovable anime protagonists
